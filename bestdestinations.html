Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is the neighbor of India, a multinational state, lies in the ocean and is known for its interesting culture and food. It also has great sights for tourists. The landscape of Sri Lanka is especially known because it has wildlife that consist of buffalos, elephants and sloth bears. Sri Lanka has great beaches to go surfing. With over 1000 kilometers of coastline adorned with beautiful beaches, a holiday in Sri Lanka is also perfect for beach lovers.

Mauritius

If you like to combine a beach holiday with trips in breathtaking nature, choose a holiday to Mauritius. Book cheap flights with TUI and travel to this beautiful bounty island located approximately 850 kilometers east of Madagascar. The snow-white beaches of Mauritius on the Indian Ocean are not only popular with sunbathers, but also with divers. The seaside resort of Trou Aux Biches is located on the popular north coast of Mauritius, which is mainly characterized by the many shipwrecks that lie off the coast.

Montenegro

Majestic mountains and breathtakingly beautiful beaches with deep blue bays, that is Montenegro. But Montenegro is much more. This country in the Balkans may be fairly small, but in terms of beauty, beaches and the amount of sights it is certainly not inferior to popular holiday countries such as Italy. The climate of Montenegro is also very pleasant: the average temperature in summer is around 26 degrees or above, ideal for a beach holiday on the Adriatic coast.
