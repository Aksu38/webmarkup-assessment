Italy

Italy has a rich history and culture , but also a coastline of 7,600 kilometers with many beautiful beaches that make the country an ideal destination for a beach holiday. From small pebble beaches in rocky coves to white sand beaches with an azure blue sea: just like the many regions and areas, the beaches in Italy are also very diverse. Also striking is the large amount of beaches that have been awarded a Bandiera Blu, or Blue Flag. In 2016, no fewer than 149 Italian beaches have been awarded this seal of approval.

Istanbul

Istanbul consists of several districts, each with its own atmosphere, character and cultural highlights. One of the most famous districts is Sultanahmet , the old center of the metropolis. There is so much to see here that it may be a little difficult to determine where to start. Although that doesn't really matter, because the sights are within walking distance of each other. A good starting point is the Hagia Sophia, very impressive both inside and out.

Canada

You can call Canada a large zoo without fences. In the romantic forests, the fast-flowing rivers and the snowy mountain areas, there is plenty of wildlife present. For example, during a holiday in Canada you can come face to face with grizzly bears that hunt salmon. Or spot orcas and whales swimming off the coast of Vancouver Island. For nature lovers, Canada is a paradise on earth. If you want to see as much of the country as possible, you can book a tour of Canada at TUI.
